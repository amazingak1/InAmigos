Quick answers to common questions about InAmigos Foundation.

We are CSR-1 registered, making us eligible for corporate social responsibility partnerships. Companies can collaborate with us on any of our six projects. Contact us at inamigosfoundation@gmail.com to discuss partnership opportunities.
