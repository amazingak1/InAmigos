About Us

Get to Know Us Better

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO). It is a Section 8 registered non-profit organisation, licensed by the Central Government, headquartered in Chhattisgarh.


We hold 80G & 12A certifications ensuring transparency and tax-exempt benefits for donors. We are also CSR-1 registered, NITI Aayog registered, and carry the prestigious IAF ISO 9001:2015 certification — reflecting our commitment to quality.

Our Initiatives

Our Key Projects

Six focused initiatives driving real, measurable change across communities in India.

Humanitarian
🍲

Project Seva

Providing food and clothing to the underprivileged, ensuring no one sleeps hungry or feels unseen in their struggle.

Education
📚

Project Bachpanshala

Ensuring quality education for underprivileged children — nurturing young minds and building bright, hopeful futures.

Animal Welfare
🐾

Project Jeev

Animal welfare through rescue, protection, and feeding. Because compassion has no boundaries — it extends to all living beings.

Empowerment
🦋

Project Udaan

Women empowerment through skill development and financial independence — helping them soar to a brighter future.

Environment
🌿

Project Prakriti

Environmental conservation and sustainability efforts — planting trees, cleaning rivers, and protecting our planet for future generations.

Skills
🚀

Project Vikas

Enhancing employability through skill development programs — bridging the gap between talent and opportunity across India.
