Founder & CEO

Mr. Govind Shukla

Founder & Chief Executive Officer, InAmigos Foundation

Mr. Govind Shukla founded InAmigos Foundation on September 23, 2020, with a powerful vision: to create a compassionate India where no one is left behind. Under his leadership, the foundation has grown from a local initiative to a nationwide movement spanning 28 states, impacting over 50,000 lives through six transformative projects.

His dedication to transparency, quality, and social impact has earned the foundation prestigious certifications including ISO 9001:2015, NITI Aayog registration, and CSR-1 status — making InAmigos a trusted partner for corporate and individual philanthropy.
